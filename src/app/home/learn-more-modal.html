What is UniVote?

UniVote is a modern digital voting platform designed specifically for student campus elections.

Why it was built: To increase student engagement in campus elections and make voting more accessible to everyone.

How it works:

  • Register with your student credentials
  • Browse candidates and their platforms
  • Cast your vote securely
  • View real-time results

Secure Login with 2FA
